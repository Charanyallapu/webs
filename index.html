QR code generator for websites

Need a QR code that links straight to a website? Select the URL / Link tab above, paste your website address (for example https://yoursite.com), and a scannable QR code appears instantly. Anyone who scans it with their phone camera is taken directly to your site — no app required.

This is one of the most common uses for QR codes: adding them to business cards, flyers, posters, restaurant menus, packaging, or storefront windows so people can visit your website without typing a long URL. Since the code is generated as a static QR code, it will keep working indefinitely as long as your website URL stays the same.

For best results when printing a website QR code, use the High or Max error-correction setting if you plan to add a logo in the center, and always test-scan the downloaded PNG before printing in bulk.
